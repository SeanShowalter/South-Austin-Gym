About Us

Martial arts, self defense, fitness – you can get it all at South Austin Gym. The synergistic nature of our programs makes it easy to move from one practice to the next. Gyms in Austin can’t compete with our programs.

Ditch Unispiring Gyms

South Austin Gym is the anti-big box gym, which is why our members love our facility. It’s unlike all other gyms in Austin, TX because we don’t just supply you with workout equipment. Every one who steps through our door is going to get motivation, support and the resources they need to live a healthier, stronger life.

Why Choose Us?

South Austin Gym is a full-service, community-oriented fitness facility in the heart of vibrant South Austin. We offer mixed-martial arts classes and personal training along with a tailored nutrition program to meet your goals.

Our boxing, kickboxing and jiu-jitsu programs are designed for competitors. Unlike many martial arts schools, you can actually spar and compete at our gym if you’d like, but you don’t have to. Our members have a variety of reasons to join, from competition,, fitness, to self-defense.

Come join us and become South Austin Strong!

Our Instructors

The expert instructors at our premier boxing gym in Austin are the heart and soul of our establishment. They are master trainers, certified in their specialties and full of real world experience. Our coaches have up to 300+ fights under their belts!

Under the leadership of former World Champion Randy Palmer, our instructors provide personalized training in:

  • Kickboxing
  • Dutch Muay Thai/Leithwei
  • SAS Boxing
  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • General Health and Wellness

Our Facility

Unlike other generic Austin gyms our facility is built out for general fitness and specialized training in a number of disciplines. We have all of the capabilities of a big gym, but other gyms in Austin aren’t set up to offer what we provide.

Our facility includes the following amenities:

  • A fully equipped weight room
  • Full-size boxing ring
  • Full locker rooms with showers
  • Pro Shop with training gear and beverages
  • Huge selection of exercise and cardiovascular machines
  • Personal trainers that are certified and experienced in the ring
  • State-of-the-art boxing/martial arts training facility and equipment

Take Part in Special Events

South Austin Gym is also a community event center. Gym members, fighters and locals can sit ringside during amateur or pro matches in several of the sports that we teach.
